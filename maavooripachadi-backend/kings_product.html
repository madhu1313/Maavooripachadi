Raagi Murukulu – Crispy, Wholesome & Guilt-Free Snacking

Discover the perfect balance of taste and health with our Raagi Murukulu, a nutritious twist on the classic South Indian snack. Made from finger millet (raagi) flour blended with aromatic spices and deep-fried to golden perfection, these crunchy spirals are a wholesome way to satisfy your snack cravings.

Rich in calcium, fiber, and essential nutrients, Raagi Murukulu is an ideal choice for health-conscious snackers who don't want to compromise on flavor. Enjoy them with your evening tea or serve them as a festive treat—every bite is packed with traditional taste and modern nutrition.

  • Made with nutrient-rich finger millet (raagi)
  • Infused with traditional South Indian spices
  • Crispy texture with a wholesome twist
  • No artificial colors or preservatives
  • Perfect for everyday snacking or festive spreads

Allergen Info: May contain traces of gluten

Crunchy, healthy, and full of flavor – bring home the taste of tradition!
